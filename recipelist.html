Momo Recipe

Mix minced meat or vegetables with spices for filling. Prepare dough, cut into small circles. Stuff with filling, seal edges creating pleats. Steam for 10 minutes. Serve hot with spicy tomato-based achar. Enjoy this delectable Himalayan treat!

Pizza Recipe

Prepare dough and let it rise. Roll out, spread tomato sauce, sprinkle cheese, and add toppings of choice. Bake in a hot oven until crust is golden and cheese melts. Serve hot. Slice, enjoy the deliciousness!

Burger Recipe

Season beef patty and grill until desired doneness. Toast a bun, spread condiments. On the bottom bun, place lettuce, tomato, the patty, cheese, and any preferred toppings. Top with the other bun half. Serve immediately. Relish the juicy bite!

Biryani Recipe

Marinate meat in spices and yogurt. Fry onions until crispy. Cook the meat until tender. Parboil rice with spices. Layer rice, meat, and onions in a pot. Seal and cook on low heat. Garnish with coriander. Serve hot. Enjoy the flavorful layers!
